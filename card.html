PIGS

Pigs are very inteligent beings, the average pig being smarter than young human children of at least 3 years of age.

  • Scientific name: Sus scrofa domesticus
  • Average Length: 0.9 - 1.8 m
  • Average Lifespan: 6 - 10 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated

Pigs are very similar to humans in many ways. They are highly inteligent, they form complex social networks and have excellent memories. Pigs are affectionate and very peaceful beings.
